2. POLICE

My second ambition is to work as a police officer in Malaysia. I believe there are too many criminal cases in Malaysia, which is why I want to become a police officer. I consequently told myself that we must take immediate action to prevent this issue. It's not an easy job to be a responsible and trained police officer.

As a result, the training provided at the police training centre really interests me. The shooting practice was one of the workouts that drew my attention. I was curious to see how the pistol would function, so I was drawn to the exercise. Additionally, the right target must be identified for shooting instruction. The most effective training for combating the rising crime rate is provided by the Royal Malaysian Police.
